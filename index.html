Overview

A powerful web application built to work on mobile, tablet and desktop devices.

  • Sign up

    Whether you're a personal trainer or a fitness junkie, joining is easy.

  • Create exercise plans

    Give it a name, add rounds with sets, add custom exercises and breaks.

  • Get active

    Once you have created your workout, get moving and smash through it.

  • Share workouts

    Send workout plans to friends, family or students or publish them online.

Features

All the good things packed in one small application

  • Smart timer

    A timer that will notify you so you don't need to keep checking the time.

  • Discover profiles

    View other profiles and there workout plans for inspiration.

  • Workout assistant

    Focus more on your workout and let the assistant speak out.

  • Public workouts

    All workouts are public and can be played anonymously.

  • No distractions

    Listen to your favourite music while performing the exercises.

  • Universal platform

    No app required. Just visit gymphy.co on a mobile, tablet or desktop device.
